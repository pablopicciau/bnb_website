Welcome

A home in the city's most beautiful quarter

Lamarmora 18 is a small, family-run bed & breakfast in the heart of Castello, the fortified quarter overlooking Cagliari from above. We have just two rooms, each with a private bathroom: few rooms, a lot of care. We welcome you personally, share our favourite restaurants and sights, and leave you free to feel at home.

Step out of the front door and you're already in the most authentic Cagliari: artisan workshops, panoramic terraces over the gulf, historic cafés — and Poetto beach just fifteen minutes away.

Frequently asked questions

Good to know

What are the check-in and check-out times?

Check-in is from 3:00 PM to 8:00 PM; just let us know if you need a different time. Check-out is by 10:30 AM.

Do the rooms have a private bathroom?

Yes, both rooms have a private bathroom with shower, hairdryer and toiletries.

Is breakfast included?

Yes, breakfast is always included: sweet and savoury, with fresh produce and some Sardinian specialities.

Where can I park?

Castello is a limited-traffic zone: we recommend the public car parks nearby (viale Regina Elena and via Cammino Nuovo). We'll give you full directions when you book.

How far is the beach?

Poetto beach — 8 km of white sand — is about 6 km away: 15 minutes by bus (PF/PQ line) or 10 by car.

Are pets allowed?

Unfortunately we cannot accommodate pets at the moment.
